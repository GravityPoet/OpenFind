# Trademark Policy

This policy covers the OpenFind name, logo, icon, and confusingly similar marks
("OpenFind Marks").

The software code in this repository is licensed under `AGPL-3.0-only` unless a
separate commercial license applies. The AGPL grants copyright permissions for
covered files, but it does not grant trademark rights in the OpenFind Marks as
brand identifiers. No trademark rights are granted unless a separate written
agreement explicitly grants them.

## Permitted Uses

You may use the OpenFind Marks without separate permission to:

- make truthful, non-misleading references to the OpenFind project;
- state that your work is based on, compatible with, or forked from OpenFind;
- link to the official OpenFind repository;
- preserve existing notices required by the AGPL.

## Restricted Uses

You may not use the OpenFind Marks in a way that:

- implies endorsement, sponsorship, partnership, or official status without
  written permission;
- names a fork, product, service, package, domain, social account, or app store
  listing in a confusingly similar way;
- removes or alters OpenFind branding in a way that misrepresents origin;
- uses the OpenFind logo or icon for a competing commercial product without
  written permission.

## Forks and Modified Versions

Forks and modified versions should use a clearly different product name and
visual identity. A truthful attribution such as "Forked from OpenFind" is
allowed.

## Commercial Licenses

A commercial license does not include trademark rights unless the signed
commercial agreement explicitly says so.

If any part of this policy conflicts with rights that cannot be withheld under
the AGPL for AGPL-covered code, the AGPL controls for that code. This policy is
intended to reserve trademark rights, not to add restrictions to the AGPL-covered
software license.
